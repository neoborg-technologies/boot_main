Embedded Systems

We specialize in AVR and ARM microcontrollers and their interfacing techniques.

Embedded systems trainings covers wide array of topics from microcontroller details to the different interfacing techniques, these can be tailored as per requirements.

  • Getting started with AVR:-AVR microcontrollers are easy to use,consume low power and offer high level of integration, AVR 8-bit and 32-bit microcontrollers deliver combination of performance, power efficiency and design flexibility.
  • Getting started with ARM:-ARM microcontrollers are based on reduced instruction set computing (RISC) architecture.RISC based design helps to reduce costs, heat and power.
  • Circuit design and simulation techniques:We will talk about different types of circuit design and simulation techniques.
  • Guidance for Embedded Projects:Guidance will be provided,to all, for projects.

Cloud Computing

Cloud computing, the buzz word in information technology, has changed the way Data centers operated and the ways in which infrastructure was set up.

We have trainers with industry experience in the cloud computing domain thus providing practical knowledge with theoritcal concepts, this helps in clarifying doubts and gives an insight of real world scenarios.

  • IAAS:-Infrastructure As A Service is a technique of providing datacenter infrastructure without worrying about the numerous issues related to maintaining a datacenter such as compute,networking,storage, security etc.
  • PAAS:-Platform As A service is used where a part from Infrastructure OS services, virtualization etc are managed by the cloud service provider, developers need only to worry about their applications.
  • SAAS:Software As A Service, in this all is taken care of by the cloud service provider, customer uses the software for which he has paid for.
