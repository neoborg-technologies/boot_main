About Us

We are a team of individuals sharing a common passion for embedded systems, which empowers us to live, dream and develop solutions to simplify lives. This madness led us to start Neoborg where we share with who so ever is willing to learn and develop automated solutions.Neoborgians are engineers from computers, electronics and electrical field, diversifying the team on different technologies. The team at Neoborg has loads of experience on different aspects of technologies and its implementation.

We deliver trainings to organizations , universities and colleges , trainings are designed according to the needs and expertise of trainees. Content of training is modified from time to time so as to cover latest in technology and give insight to upcoming technology. This enables trainees to stay current with respect to technology and be future ready. We not only deliver but also provide right platform and tools to trainees so as to enable them to keep practicing. We strive to excel in what we deliver and help trainees excel with us. Neoborgians believe in " practice , practice, practice till you are expert in what you do and then move forward,again to practice , learn and acquire new horizons".

Mission

Impart knowledge in futuristic technologies to nurture talent and manufacture high end automation kits and products using advance technology and continous R&D.

Vision

Leverage technology to educate,automate and improve the way humanity lives.
